*Last updated: April 2026*

## 30-day money-back guarantee

We offer a **30-day full refund** on all purchases, no questions asked.

If RemoteX Pro does not meet your needs for any reason, contact us within 30 days of your purchase date and we will issue a full refund.

## How to request a refund

Email [flelard@gmail.com](mailto:flelard@gmail.com) with:

- The email address used for your purchase
- Your order number (from your LemonSqueezy receipt)

Refunds are processed within 5 business days.

## After a refund

Upon refund, your Pro license key will be deactivated and Pro features will no longer be accessible. Your buttons and configuration are not deleted — you keep full access to the free tier.

## Renewals (yearly license)

Yearly license renewals are eligible for a refund within 14 days of the renewal date, provided the license has not been used on more than one device since renewal.
